ABOUT US


Welcome to SAVARKAR GROUND, where cricket dreams come to life. Established in 2019, we are dedicated to providing top-notch facilities and an unparalleled cricketing experience for players and fans alike. Our state-of-the-art ground features world-class pitches, modern amenities, and a vibrant atmosphere that captures the spirit of the game. Whether you're here to play, watch, or simply soak in the excitement, we ensure every visit is unforgettable. Join us in celebrating the passion, tradition, and future of cricket at SAVARKAR GROUND.

Tournaments

At SAVARKAR GROUND, experience cricket's pinnacle with international clashes, local leagues, and vibrant community events. From thrilling T20 battles to youth championships, our ground hosts a spectrum of tournaments, uniting players and fans in the spirit of competition and camaraderie. Welcome to cricketing excellence at SAVARKAR GROUND.

Training

At SAVARKAR GROUND, elevate your game with expert cricket lessons. Our qualified coaches offer personalized training for all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned players. Perfect your technique, enhance your strategy, and unlock your potential on the pitch. Join us and take your cricket skills to the next level.

Canteen

Refuel and recharge at the SAVARKAR GROUND canteen, where delicious bites meet cricketing excitement. From classic matchday snacks to hearty meals, our menu satisfies every craving. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere as you grab a bite between innings or linger with fellow fans. Taste the essence of cricketing delight.
